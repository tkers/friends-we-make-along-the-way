Friends We Make Along the Way

A nautical TTRPG for 1-4 players

Made for #1prpgjam 2022. Licensed under the MIT license.

How to play Start with a blank piece of paper, and make sure all players have a pencil within reach.

Suits Depending on which card you draw, your discovery is...

♠ a natural phenomenon
♦ a structure ashore
♣ a creature of the sea
♥ a ship passing by
